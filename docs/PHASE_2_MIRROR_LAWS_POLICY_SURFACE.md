# Phase 2 Mirror Laws Policy Surface

This document records the addition of the first explicit Mirror Laws policy surface in Eidonic Core.

## Purpose
Define the governing Mirror Laws in clear system terms before any runtime enforcement is introduced.

## What changed
- added `docs/MIRROR_LAWS_POLICY.md`

## Why this matters
- governance language should not remain folklore
- future Guardian Protocol work needs a prior rule surface
- the system should define governing constraints before trying to enforce them

## Current truth
The Mirror Laws now exist as explicit policy only.

They are not yet runtime-enforced.
They do not yet block live orchestration behavior.
They will inform future governance eval, provenance, and Guardian Protocol surfaces.
